About

Life is about balance…. the tamed and the untamed, the rooted and the wild. I find happiness in the balance of nature. The union of opposites inspires me in my day-to-day life and creativity.

Rooted & Wild was created out of my wild eye for all things floral, botanical, and getting lost somewhere in between the ocean and mountains. With a mindful nod to these passions – along with importance of moments, expressions and people – my florals are a direct representation of my heart and soul.

With a specialty focus on events and weddings, my art will in turn reflect natural balances that create lasting impressions.

I am so excited to meet you and share my joy with you. Lets talk flowers for your next event or photoshoot… and also life, art and nature over coffee or a good IPA.
